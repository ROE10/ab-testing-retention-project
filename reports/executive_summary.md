
EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
=================

Project: A/B Test: Should We Offer a 15% Discount to Reduce Customer Churn?

Business Context:
- Subscription-based e-commerce platform considering a 15% discount to reduce churn.

Key Findings:

Overall Churn:
- Control Group Churn Rate: 0.1220 (~12.20%)
- Treatment Group Churn Rate: 0.1027 (~10.27%)
- Retention Lift: 0.0192 (~1.92% reduction in churn)

Net Revenue:
- Average Net Revenue Control: $843.48
- Average Net Revenue Treatment: $735.17
- Total Net Revenue Control: $8,437,311.48
- Total Net Revenue Treatment: $7,349,537.68
- Incremental Net Revenue: $-1,087,773.80

Segment Analysis:
- High Segment: Control Churn 0.0879, Treatment Churn 0.0593
- Low Segment: Control Churn 0.1642, Treatment Churn 0.1622
- Mid Segment: Control Churn 0.1145, Treatment Churn 0.0886


Recommendation:
- Offer the 15% discount to high-CLV customers only, maximizing retention and revenue.
- Monitor mid-CLV segment in a follow-up experiment.
